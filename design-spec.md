### Why Wire Mesh Reinforced Floor
- Tractor wheel point loads: ~2-3 tonnes per wheel
- Wire mesh distributes load, prevents cracking
- 6mm Fe500 bars at 150mm (6") centers both ways
- 4" slab thickness adequate for this loading
- Alternative: 8mm bars at 200mm if heavier equipment expected

### Floor Finish Options

| Option | Cost | Durability | Maintenance |
|--------|------|------------|-------------|
| IPS (Indian Patent Stone) | Medium | High | Needs polish every 2-3 yrs |
| Plain cement with hardener | Low | Medium | Can crack under wheels |
| Kota stone | High | Very high | Almost zero |
| Tremix (VDF) flooring | Medium-High | Very high | Best for heavy loads |

**Recommendation:** IPS flooring (cement + marble chips, machine polished). Strong, good-looking, easy to clean farm products. The wire mesh in the base slab handles structural load regardless of finish.

## 16. Construction Sequence

1. **Site preparation** вҖ” clear vegetation, mark layout with lime/thread
2. **Excavation** вҖ” dig column footing pits (3ft Г— 3ft Г— 3.5ft deep each)
3. **PCC bed** вҖ” pour 6" M15 concrete in each pit, level, cure 2 days
4. **Footings** вҖ” RCC footings (12" thick, 3ft Г— 3ft), cure 7 days
5. **Column stubs** вҖ” raise column bars from footing to plinth level (+3ft)
6. **Plinth walls** вҖ” 9" brick walls between columns up to +3ft
7. **Earth filling** вҖ” fill inside plinth in 6" layers, water, compact each layer
8. **PCC over fill** вҖ” 3-4" M15 concrete, level, cure
9. **Floor slab** вҖ” lay wire mesh, pour 4" M20 concrete, cure 14 days
10. **Columns** вҖ” cast columns from +3ft to +15ft (in 2 lifts if needed)
11. **Walls** вҖ” raise 9" external walls + 4.5" internal walls
12. **Leave openings** вҖ” shutter, gate, vents, toilet exhaust
13. **Cast lintels** вҖ” RCC lintels at all openings + future window positions
14. **Build staircase** вҖ” dog-legged, cast waist slabs + steps in one pour
15. **Beam formwork** вҖ” install formwork for all beams at +15ft level
16. **Slab formwork** вҖ” formwork for entire ceiling slab including 5ft cantilever
17. **Slab steel** вҖ” lay reinforcement (main + distribution bars + cantilever bars)
18. **Concrete pour** вҖ” pour entire slab in one go (M20, avoid cold joints)
19. **Curing** вҖ” wet cure slab for minimum 21 days
20. **Plumbing** вҖ” install pipes in walls (before plastering)
21. **Plastering** вҖ” cement plaster (1:4 inside, 1:6 outside)
22. **Flooring** вҖ” IPS or chosen finish on ground floor
23. **Toilet finishing** вҖ” tiles, commode, basin, connections
24. **External works** вҖ” ramp, steps, soak pit, water tank
25. **Painting** вҖ” white weatherproof paint (external), distemper (internal)
26. **Installations** вҖ” shutter, gate, grills, handrails, electrical (if any)
