4.4 Load Path / भार मार्ग

Roof Slab → Beams (Deep beams at z=9 and z=25) → Columns (C1-C8) → Foundation

Water Tank load → Roof Slab → Beams → Columns

Partition wall (4.5" brick) sits on middle deep beam — not a load-bearing wall itself.

Important / ध्यान दें:
  • All columns must be continuous from foundation to roof (no breaks at floor level)
  • Roof slab must have 1.5% slope toward front for water drainage
  • Stair well opening (3ft × 8ft) requires trimmer beams around the opening
  • Cantilever (terrace) must have top reinforcement extended 2× cantilever length into slab

7. Construction Notes / ठेकेदार के लिए निर्देश

7.1 Sequence of Work / काम का क्रम

  1. Column extension (GF to Roof): C3, C4, C5, C8 columns must be cast continuously from 1F floor level to roof slab level (12ft). Use column box formwork. Ensure vertical alignment with GF columns using plumb bob.
  2. Wall construction: Build external walls (9") first, then partition wall on middle beam, then internal walls (bathroom, kitchen). Leave door/window openings with proper lintel provision.
  3. Bathroom walls: Build bathroom walls (4.5") straddling the partition line — 4ft inside room + 4ft into balcony zone. Waterproof the floor BEFORE tiling.
  4. Kitchen half-wall: Build 4.5" wall to 3.5ft height only. Cap with 4" slab/granite top.
  5. Beam & slab formwork: Erect formwork for roof beams and slab. Middle deep beam (9"×20") is critical — ensure adequate propping below.
  6. Steel fixing: Fix reinforcement as per structural drawing. Ensure minimum cover (1.5" for slab, 2" for beams, 2" for columns).
  7. Slab casting: Cast entire roof slab in one pour (20ft × 25ft). Vibrate properly. Cure for minimum 14 days.
  8. Stair slab: Cast sloped slab (3.5ft × 9ft, 53°) after roof slab has cured. Anchor properly into partition beam and roof slab.
  9. Waterproofing (roof): After curing, apply waterproofing treatment. Ensure 1.5% slope toward front with PCC.
  10. Plastering: External double coat (12mm + 8mm), internal single coat (12mm). Cure each coat.
  11. Electrical & plumbing: Concealed conduit and pipes before plastering. Test plumbing pressure before closing walls.
  12. Tiling & finishing: Floor tiles after all wet work complete. Wall tiles in bathroom/kitchen first, then POP punning, then painting.

7.2 Critical Points / महत्वपूर्ण बातें

#PointReason / कारण
1Columns C5, C8 must extend full height to roofWithout these, roof slab at front has no support. 20ft span cannot be free.
2Middle deep beam (9"×20") at z=9 is the most important beamCarries partition wall + room slab + balcony slab loads. Under-sizing will cause deflection.
3Stair well opening needs trimmer beams3ft×8ft hole in slab weakens it. Trimmer beams redistribute load around opening.
4Bathroom waterproofing before any tilingLeakage into GF is very expensive to fix later. Use membrane + liquid waterproofing.
5Roof slope must go toward FRONT (south)Rainwater drains toward road side. No water pooling on roof.
6Terrace has NO roof above — railing onlyDo not build any wall or slab over the 5ft terrace portion. It is the cantilever from GF slab.
7Sloped stair slab: ensure adequate anchorage53° is steep. The slab must be properly anchored at both ends (beam at bottom, roof slab at top).
8All concealed pipes/wires BEFORE plasteringChasing walls after plaster wastes time and weakens walls.

7.3 Contractor Checklist / चेकलिस्ट

  • ☐ Column reinforcement checked and photographed before pouring
  • ☐ Beam dimensions verified (especially middle deep beam 9"×20")
  • ☐ Slab reinforcement: spacing, cover, overlap length checked
  • ☐ Stair well opening size confirmed (3ft × 8ft) with trimmer beams
  • ☐ Plumbing pressure test passed (bathroom, kitchen supply lines)
  • ☐ Electrical conduit continuity tested before plastering
  • ☐ Bathroom floor waterproofing tested (48-hour ponding test)
  • ☐ Roof slope verified (water should flow toward front edge)
  • ☐ Roof waterproofing applied and tested
  • ☐ All windows/doors: lintels cast before wall construction above
  • ☐ MS railing welded, painted with red oxide + enamel (2 coats)
  • ☐ Water tank platform and pipe connections completed
