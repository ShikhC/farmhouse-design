5. AC Provision Detail (एसी प्रावधान)

Indoor Unit (इनडोर यूनिट)

  • Position: Left wall of living/bedroom, 7-8ft from floor
  • Capacity: 1.5T or 2T split AC (inverter type recommended)
  • Power: Dedicated 20A MCB, 4 sq mm wire (L+N+E)
  • Socket: 16A dedicated socket behind indoor unit OR hardwired
  • Drain: Condensate drain pipe to nearest outlet (bathroom/balcony)

Outdoor Unit (आउटडोर यूनिट)

  • Position: Balcony wall bracket (left side) OR on open terrace
  • Bracket: MS powder-coated wall bracket, 2ft from floor
  • Clearance: Min 12" from wall for airflow
  • Foundation: Anti-vibration pads on bracket

Pipe Sleeve & Connections

  • Wall sleeve: 3" (75mm) PVC pipe through partition wall
  • Slope: Slight outward slope for condensate drainage
  • Contents through sleeve:
    • Refrigerant copper pipes (liquid + gas)
    • Inter-connecting cable
    • Condensate drain pipe
  • Sealing: Putty/foam seal after installation
  • Height: Same as indoor unit (7-8ft from floor)

Electrical for AC

  • Wire: 4 sq mm FR copper (L+N+E) in 25mm conduit
  • MCB: 20A DP (Double Pole) C-curve
  • Separate from other circuits in DB
  • Stabilizer: External, if voltage fluctuation area

9. Contractor Notes — Work Sequence (ठेकेदार निर्देश — कार्य क्रम)

9.1 Construction Sequence (कार्य अनुक्रम)

Step Activity Details (विवरण) Check Before Next Step
1 Conduit in Walls
(दीवारों में नाली)		 Chase walls for conduit runs. Embed 20mm/25mm UPVC conduits. Pull GI draw wire. Plug/plaster chases. Conduit from DB location to all switch/socket points. Pressure test conduits (blow air). Ensure no kinks/blocks. Photo-document before plastering.
2 Conduit in Slab
(छत में नाली)		 Before slab pour: lay conduit for light/fan points on slab formwork. Install fan hook boxes. Install junction boxes at fixture locations. Verify all conduit connections. Fan hooks properly embedded. No conduit crushed.
3 AC Sleeve
(एसी स्लीव)		 Install 3" PVC sleeve through left wall (partition/external) at 7-8ft height. Slight outward slope (5 degrees). Verify sleeve is clear, not blocked by plaster. Correct position for indoor/outdoor unit.
4 Wall Plastering
(प्लास्टर)		 Complete wall plastering. Conduit embedded and covered. Switch/socket boxes flush with plaster surface. All boxes level and flush. No conduit exposed.
5 False Ceiling Frame
(फॉल्स सीलिंग फ्रेम)		 Install GI channel framework for gypsum FC in living/bedroom. Install PVC FC frame in kitchen/bathroom. Mark and cut openings for fan drop rods. Level check. Frame strong enough for fixtures. Fan rod holes aligned with slab hooks.
6 Wire Pulling
(तार खींचना)		 Pull all cables through conduits using GI wire. Connect at junction boxes. Leave adequate loop at fixtures/switches (8-10" extra). Label all wires at DB end. Continuity test (multimeter) every circuit. Insulation resistance test. No wire damage.
7 FC Board Installation
(बोर्ड लगाना)		 Fix gypsum/PVC boards on frame. Cut openings for LED panels, downlighters, AC vents, fan canopies. Finish joints (putty for gypsum). Openings correct size. No wires pinched. Boards secured.
8 DB Installation
(DB लगाना)		 Mount DB box on partition wall. Connect all circuit wires to respective MCBs. Connect neutral bar, earth bar. Label every MCB clearly. Tighten all terminal screws (torque). Verify phase-neutral correct. Earth continuity.
9 Fixture Installation
(फिक्सचर लगाना)		 Install all LED panels, tubes, bulbs, fans, switches, sockets. Connect and test each point individually with DB live. Every point works. Fans spin correct direction. No sparking. Socket polarity check.
10 Testing & Commissioning
(टेस्टिंग)		 Full load test. ELCB trip test (press test button). Insulation resistance measurement. Earth loop impedance. Verify 2-way stair switch from both positions. ELCB trips within 30ms at 30mA. All circuits within safe limits. Issue test certificate.

9.2 Important Safety Notes (सुरक्षा निर्देश)

  • All wire ISI marked + FR grade (IS 694) — no compromise
  • Earth wire mandatory on every circuit — green sleeve
  • No joints inside conduit — joints only in junction boxes with proper connectors
  • Maximum 3 wires in 20mm conduit, 4 wires in 25mm conduit
  • Bathroom: No socket below 4ft height. IP44 minimum for all fittings. Zone rules as per IS 732
  • Geyser: Must have dedicated MCB + indicator. Metal body earthed.
  • AC circuit: 4 sq mm wire mandatory. DP MCB (both L+N break)
  • Fan hooks: Direct in RCC slab — NOT in false ceiling frame
  • Switch height: 4.5ft from floor (standard). Socket: 1-1.5ft (unless counter-height specified)
  • DB access: Must remain accessible at all times. No furniture blocking.
  • Labelling: Label every MCB with circuit name (Hindi + English)
  • Spare conduits: Leave 1-2 spare conduit runs for future (CCTV, smart home, etc.)

9.3 Coordination Points (समन्वय बिंदु)

Plumber (प्लंबर): Coordinate geyser water inlet/outlet position with electrical point. Washing machine drain + water point adjacent to electrical socket. AC condensate drain routing.

Mason (राजमिस्त्री): Wall chasing for conduits before plaster. Slab conduit before pour. AC sleeve during wall construction.

Carpenter/FC (बढ़ई/FC): FC frame before wire pulling. Fan hole positions. LED panel cutout sizes. Cove dimensions for LED strips.

Painter (पेंटर): Wall painting after switch/socket plate installation (use painter's tape on plates). FC painting before fixture install.
