## Tips for Consistency in Google Veo

1. **Always include the building description** — the [BUILDING: ...] block at the start of each prompt ensures Veo generates the SAME structure
2. **Key features to ALWAYS mention** (or Veo will omit them):
   - "3ft brown brick PLINTH" (raised platform)
   - "10ft dark rolling SHUTTER" (not a door)
   - "3ft mahogany GATE with 5 STEPS"
   - "12ft × 8ft concrete RAMP" (sloping, textured)
   - "5ft CHAJJA/cantilever shade (NO columns underneath)"
   - "Blue 500L water TANK on roof"
   - "MS pipe RAILING" on first floor
   - "Column-free interior" (no pillars on the floor)
   - "Dog-legged staircase with HANDRAILS"
3. **Use CAPS for key features** — helps the AI prioritize those elements
4. **Specify "no columns under chajja"** — AI often adds columns by default
5. **Mention "Indian farmland / North India"** — sets the correct environment
6. **Keep camera descriptions separate** from building descriptions for clarity
